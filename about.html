Jostein Hodne

Jostein Hodne is an 18-year old acoustic fingerstyle guitarist based in Trondheim, Norway. He has performed alongside internationally renowned musicians such as Livio Gianola, and has received praise from many leading figures in the guitar world such as Jon Gomm, Robin Sukroso, Federico Casagrande and Szandor Szabo. Following his first-place at the 2016 Akustiske Landskap guitar festival talent competition, Jostein is now ready to release his debut album, thus bringing his original compositions to the fingerstyle guitar scene. Alternate tunings, percussive techniques and expressive melodies are all trademarks of his first full length release, which will be made available in autumn 2019.
